Why the need for a strong resume?

As a fresher due to lack of work experience, your resume is the only thing that can make you stand out and help you in landing an internship. Especially for a fresher like you, you need to prove your worth to the hiring committee and hence convince them into selecting you. A professional and proper resume format with very detailed oriented contents and information thus acts as a boon for college going students and also makes up for the lack of experience and makes them come across as mature and suitable for responsibility.
